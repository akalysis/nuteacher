What I Do

I take on the statistical work that is too complex, too high-stakes, or too specialised for an in-house team to handle alone. That means leading the analysis from design through to interpretation, and making sure the results hold up to scrutiny.

I work as a fractional Chief Statistician or senior analytical lead, embedded in your project without the overhead of a permanent hire. The output is defensible insight, not fragile reporting.
