# The Newcastle 85+ Study

The Newcastle 85+ Study is a population-based longitudinal study of health and ageing in the oldest old.

## Overview

The study approached all individuals born in 1921 and registered with a general practice in Newcastle or North Tyneside. The aim was to comprehensively assess their health, biological, clinical, and psychosocial profiles.

### Key Objectives

1.  To evaluate the spectrum of health and illness in the 85-year-old population.
2.  To investigate the biological and clinical factors associated with healthy ageing.
3.  To examine the trajectories of health and decline over time.
