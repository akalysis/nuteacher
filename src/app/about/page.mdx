# About Me

Welcome to my digital garden. I am Andrew Kingston, PhD, CStat, SFHEA. 

My work focuses on the intersection of epidemiology, statistics, and gerontology. I am particularly interested in how we measure, model, and understand the complex processes of human ageing.

## Key Research Areas

*   **Longitudinal Data Analysis**: Methods for analyzing data collected over time, particularly handling missing data and attrition in older populations.
*   **Health Expectancies**: Calculating and interpreting disability-free life expectancies to understand if our longer lives are spent in good or poor health.
*   **The Newcastle 85+ Study**: Extensive involvement in this unique cohort study of individuals born in 1921.
